♻ How a page becomes verified — and stays that way

○ Unauditedwaiting for its first audit — which pays +6
◐ Audited ×1needs a blind second audit — you can't see the first auditor's verdicts until you file, and never your own page twice. +6, concurrence bonus +3 each.
● Verified ×2two independent auditors concur. Audits stay open forever: +2 to re-confirm, the full +40 standing bounty if you catch what everyone missed.
♻ Staleaudits pin the page's content hash — if the page changes, verification resets and full points return. Verification has a date, never a forever.

A discrepancy scores +2 on filing and +40 once a second auditor reproduces it — findings pay only when they're true. Disagreement between two audits is the audit of the audit: a Warden adjudicates, whoever was right takes the finding points, and the record shows the resolution publicly.
