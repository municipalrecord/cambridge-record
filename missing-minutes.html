Missing minutes

Meetings that happened, whose minutes are not published on the city's portals — 810 as of 2026-07-21. Minutes may exist unpublished; each entry is a candidate for a public records request to the City Clerk or the board's department.

Keeping minutes isn't optional: charter §9-7 and Council Rule 28 require every body to keep agendas and minutes. This page is a running audit of those obligations.

Standing gaps

  • Planning Board & BZA written public comment is never published on the portals (goes to staff email only) — records request
  • School Committee meetings before Feb 2021 are on legacy cpsd.us pages, not the portal
