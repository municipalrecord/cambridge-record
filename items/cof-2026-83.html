PENDING HOME RULE PETITIONS UPDATE – JUNE 1, 2026

HD 4706, An Act Requiring That Broker Fees in the City of Cambridge be Paid by the Party Who Solicits a Licensed Broker was filed by Representative Connolly. House - Referred to the Committee on Housing May 15, 2025. Senate – Senate concurred May 19, 2025. Joint – Hearing scheduled for November 19, 2025 from 11:00AM-5:00PM in Gardner Auditorium. House – Reported by committee to Clerk’s Office for processing, will accompany a study order.

House – Accompanied a study order, see H5352 (under House Rule 27) April 13, 2026. For legislative history: https://malegislature.gov/Bills/194/HD4706 HD 4735, An Act Further Regulating Procurements By The City of Cambridge, adopted by the City Council on March 24, 2025, was filed by Representative Decker on May 21, 2025. House – Referred to the Committee on Municipalities and Regional Government May 27, 2025. Senate – Senate concurred May 29, 2025.

Joint – Hearing scheduled for June 24, 2025 from 1:00PM-5:00PM in B-1. House – Bill reported favorably by committee and referred to the committee on Housing Steering, Policy and Scheduling July 16, 2025. House – Committee reported that the matter be placed in the Orders of the Day for the next sitting August 14, 2025. House – Rules suspended August 14, 2025. House – Read second and ordered to a third reading August 14, 2025.

For legislative history: https://malegislature.gov/Bills/194/HD4735 H.3924, An Act expanding the definition of dependent for the purposes of municipal employee benefits as to the City of Cambridge. This legislation was filed by Representative Decker. House - Referred to the Committee on Public Service on March 20, 2025. Senate – Senate concurred December 15, 2025. Joint – Hearing scheduled for January 27, 2026 from 1:00PM-5:00PM in B-1 January 17, 2026.
