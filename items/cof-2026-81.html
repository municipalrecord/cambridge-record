Development & University Relations Committee

• Councillor McGovern will be removed from the Economic Development & University Relations Committee and added to the Housing Committee Councillors Simmons and McGovern requested this change to better align their respective council work with their policy priorities. After reviewing this request, I am satisfied that this reassignment serves the best interests of the Council and the residents we represent. A full updated list of committee assignments is attached for your reference. Thank you for your continued service to the residents of Cambridge. Sincerely,
