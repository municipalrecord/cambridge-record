Attachments to Poles:

1. Attachments shall be made with proper hardware and shall only be attached with lon-corrosive metal clamps or brackets. . Banners attached to poles shall not exceed a total of 1152 square inches, approximately 18 inches x 48 inches, 24 inches x 36 inches, or 24 inches x 48. The actual size is subject to required clearances. 3. Banners cannot be attached to wood utility poles. 4. Banners shall have a minimum clearance of: a. 16 feet over a roadway b. 12 feet over a sidewalk 5.

Banners shall not interfere with or obstruct, or otherwise block any roadway signs, lighting, or traffic signals. 6. The City reserves the right to remove, or order to be removed any banner deemed unsafe, not properly installed, or creating a hazard and the applicant may be subject to costs. 7. An applicant must provide a description of the materials the banner is made of, method of attachment, and means of installation (contractor name and contact information). offh

To the Honorable City Councilors of Cambridge City Council

City Hall, 795 Massachusetts Avenue

Cambridge, MA 02139

Dear Council Members:

I am writing to respectfully request your permission to hang banners to celebrate and promote the Inman Square commercial district, as a part of the city's Inman Square Construction Mitigation efforts. The banners would be hung in the Spring of 2019, and be taken down no earlier than that Fall of 2023. The request includes twenty-five (25) 30" × 60" street pole banners to be hung on poles in the neighborhood. The banners will be double sided so they will look the same from both sides.

Enclosed are the design for the street pole banners and the exact street poles we propose to use. (The design of the street pole came from an Inman Square business, Albertine Press.) A map of street poles for both this application and the Inman Square application can be found here: Thank you so much for your consideration of this request, and for the Council's support of this neighborhood's commercial district. Sincerely,

Pardis Saffari

Senior Economic Development Manager
