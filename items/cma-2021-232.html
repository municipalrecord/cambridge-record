Other Updates

4 Testing To meet anticipated increased demand for COVID-19 testing during the holiday season, the City/CPHD are meeting with CIC on Wednesday, November 3 to plan expanding the city’s program to 5 days a week. In October, the city-run testing sites administered 11,020 tests, averaging 689 tests a session.

Case Investigation and Contact Tracing

With the closure of the state-contracted Community Tracing Collaborative in December, municipalities will assume full responsibility for case investigation and contact tracing. Cambridge, in partnership with Chelsea, Revere, and Winthrop, was awarded a state grant to hire several case investigators and contract tracers to assist the four communities.
