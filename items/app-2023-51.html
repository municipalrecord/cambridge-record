APP 2023-51

A citizens’ petition to amend the Zoning Ordinance

Zoning petitionfiled by residentsMGL c.40A §5
How it started
Filed by Allene R. Pierson and fellow residents — state law (MGL c.40A §5) lets ten registered voters put a proposed zoning change before the Council, which must process it on statutory clocks.
What happened
↪ Referred — sent to the Ordinance Committee and Planning Board, as §5 requires. (Referred to committee — unanimous · Oct 2, 2023) The Planning Board held its hearing November 21; the Ordinance Committee heard it November 29.

Voted yes (9) — unanimous

What’s next
⏳ No action taken — the city’s own zoning tracker lists this among past amendments not adopted. Under MGL c.40A §5, a petition the Council does not adopt within 90 days of its hearing lapses; adoption would have needed a two-thirds vote (6 of 9).
