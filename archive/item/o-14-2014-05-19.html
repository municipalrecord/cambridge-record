Policy Order O-14

City Council, May 19, 2014

That the City Manager is requested to confer will all relevant City departments on the feasibility of joining the FAB11 planning committee, along with co-hosts MIT and FAB Foundation, and assist in the planning process of the FAB11 Conference that will be held in Cambridge in 2015.
