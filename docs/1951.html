FAQs on City Manager Search Focus Groups 6/2/2016 rr c *All Focus Groups will be scheduled between Thursday, June 9 and Thursday, June 16 * Approximately 96 key constituency groups will be invited to participate in the Focus Groups *There will 28 Focus Groups targeting key constituency groups across the city, including two general citywide sessions that will be scheduled in two different locations of the city 4 *Two 90-minute "drop-in" sessions in City Hall for city employees will be scheduled during the course of the week * Nine one-on-one interviews with each City Council member *Approximately 16 one-on-one meetings with key staff will be interspersed into the week-long schedule *A communication plan is being developed to ensure timely and comprehensive notice of meeting , times and locations for each participating constituency group, city councillor and key staff personnel.
