To: Cambridge City Council From: Sumbul Siddiqui, Mayor Date: March 26, 2026 Subject: Update from the City Clerk Preliminary Screening Committee OFFICE OF THE MAYOR Sumbul Siddiqui Mayor (2020-21, 2022-23, 2026-27) To the Honorable, the City Council: The City Clerk search process is underway. Matt Nelson, Director of Administration & Operations, Executive Office Melissa Peters, Assistant City Manager for Community Development Naomie Stephen, Executive Assistant to the City Council I look forward to keeping you updated on the progress of this effort.
