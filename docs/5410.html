An application was received from Meghan Shaw of the Cambridge Energy Alliance, requesting permission for a temporary banner across JFK Street at Mt. Auburn Street announcing the October is Green Energy Month, 100% renewable Electricity from October 2, 2018 thru October 29, 2018

City of Cambridge Sidewalk Business Use Petition SBU-077331-2018 To the honorable, the City Council of the City of Cambridge The undersigned respectfully pray 9/25/2018 Applied: 9/25/2018 Cambridge Energy Alliance Located at Meghan Shaw 344 Broadway Cambridge, MA 02139 Name of Petitioner or Business Address Be granted permission for: The Cambridge Energy Alliance is requesting permission for a banner to be hung across JFK St at Mt Auburn St from October 2nd thru October 29th, 2018. Promoted by: 350Mass, Green Cambridge, Mother’s Out Front, Neighborhood Solar, City of Cambridge (Please note the link directs to the City of Cambridge’s official green electricity program: Cambridge Community Electricity http://masspowerchoice.com/cambridge)
