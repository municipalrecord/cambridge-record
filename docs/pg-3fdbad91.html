Congratulations to the Alibhai-Dadabhoy family on the birth of their son Agha Ibrahim Alibhai.

RES 2026-41 FIRST IN COUNCIL March 16, 2026 City of Cambridge COUNCILLOR SIMMONS COUNCILLOR FLAHERTY COUNCILLOR AL-ZUBI VICE MAYOR AZEEM COUNCILLOR MCGOVERN COUNCILLOR NOLAN COUNCILLOR SOBRINHO-WHEELER COUNCILLOR ZUSY MAYOR SIDDIQUI WHEREAS: The Cambridge City Council is delighted to join family, friends, and neighbors in extending their congratulations to Cambridge residents Dr. Yasin Alibhai and Dr. Farah Dadabhoy on the birth of their son Agha Ibrahim Alibhai on March 9, 2026; and WHEREAS: The birth of a child is a moment of great happiness and promise, bringing new love and excitement to a family and strengthening the fabric of our community; and WHEREAS: The City Council also celebrates Suleiman, who now embraces the important and exciting role of big brother as the family welcomes its newest member; and WHEREAS: The Cambridge community takes pride in recognizing and celebrating the milestones and joys of its residents, including the arrival of the newest generation who will grow, learn, and contribute to the life of our city; now therefore be it RESOLVED: That the City Council extends its warmest congratulations and best wishes to Dr. Yasin Alibhai, Dr. Farah Dadabhoy, Suleiman, and their growing family on the birth of their new child, and wishes them a lifetime filled with love, health, and happiness; and be it further RESOLVED: That the City Clerk be and hereby is requested to forward a suitably engrossed copy of this resolution to the Alibhai-Dadabhoy Family on behalf of the entire City Council.
