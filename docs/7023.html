That the City Manager is requested to direct the Department of Public Works to pay additional attention to the cleaning and maintenance of Greene-Rose Heritage Park for the balance of the summer season and going forward

City of Cambridge O-1 ORIGINAL ORDER IN CITY COUNCIL July 30, 2019 COUNCILLOR SIMMONS WHEREAS: A number of residents have recently reached out to the City Council citing an increased amount of garbage and debris in and around Greene-Rose Heritage Park, and have requested that the City make greater efforts to maintain the cleanliness of this area; now therefore be it ORDERED: That the City Manager be and hereby is requested to direct the Department of Public Works to pay additional attention to the cleaning and maintenance of Greene-Rose Heritage Park for the balance of the summer season and going forward, and to report back on this effort to the City Council this fall.
