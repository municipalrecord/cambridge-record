A communication transmitted from Louis A. DePasquale, City Manager, relative to Awaiting Report Item Number 21-79, regarding proposed amendments to Article 8.12 of the Municipal Code

That Chapter 8.12.010 of the Cambridge Municipal Code be amended to delete section 8.12.010(B). That Chapter 8.12 of the Cambridge Municipal Code be amended to insert a new section to read as follows: 8.12.030 - Mobile On-Demand Fueling Service to read as follows:Fuel Dispensing System Warning Labels Each fuelgasoline dispensing system at commercial service stations in the City of Cambridge shall display a clear warning label explaining that burning gasoline, diesel and ethanol has major consequences on human health and on the environment, including contributing to climate change. The text, image, or graphics of this warning label shall be approved by the City Manager. Formatted: Indent: First line: 0"
