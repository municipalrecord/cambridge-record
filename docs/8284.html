Letter from Mayor Sumbul Siddiqui regarding COVID-19 Student Supports and Resources List

From Mayor Sumbul Siddiqui regarding COVID-19 Student Supports and Resources List·Council meeting Mar 16, 2020·1 page·📄 Original PDF (city portal)·sha256 d0de5bdafdcc…
CITY OF CAMBRIDGE OFFICE OF THE MAYOR Sumbul Siddiqui, Mayor ssiddiqui@cambridgema.gov Office: [phone removed] TTY/TTD: [phone removed] March 12, 2020 Based on the potential severity of COVID-19 outbreak in the student community, several local universities have made the difficult decision to close indefinitely to reduce possible virus exposure. While there are currently no known cases of COVID-19 in the Cambridge community, my office is committed to helping our residents and sharing information when available. Below is a list of resources specifically to support students who may be displaced due to campus closures in Cambridge. We will do our best to update this page as more resources become available.
