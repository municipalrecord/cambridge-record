DePasquale, City Manager Re: Special Meeting of the City Council for the purpose of considering an amendment to the Zoning Ordinance of the City of Cambridge by adding a Section 13.100 that creates a new PUD-8 District Councillors: By this notice I am hereby calling a special meeting of the Cambridge City Council on Tuesday, November 26, 2019, at 5:00 p.m. in the Sullivan Chamber, City Hall, for the purpose of discussing a petition by Stephen R. Karp, Trustee of Cambridgeside Galleria Associates Trust, to amend the Zoning Ordinance of the City of Cambridge by adding a Section 13.100 that creates a new PUD-8 District. Best Regards, Marc C.
