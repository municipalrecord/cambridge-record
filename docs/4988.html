May 14, 2018 To the Honorable Members of The Cambridge City Council City Hall, 795 Massachusetts Avenue Cambridge, MA 02139 Dear Council Members, I am writing to respectfully request your permission to hang a banner to help promote Find It Cambridge, the online resource that helps families in Cambridge easily find the activities, services, and resources they are looking for. The banner would be hung from July 23rd, 2018 to August 6th, 2018 and October 15th, 2018 to October 22nd , 2018 This remind parents that they can visit Find It Cambridge before the start of the school year. The request includes one double-sided cross-street banner that will be 20’ x 3.5’. The banner will be hung across 795 Massachusetts Avenue. Thank you for your consideration of this request, and for the Council’s support of Find It Cambridge Respectfully, Hector Acevedo Find It Cambridge Manager [phone removed]
