Resolution on the death of James Monagle's mother Kathleen M. Monagle

RES 2018 #121·Council meeting Apr 2, 2018·1 page·📄 Original PDF (city portal)·sha256 29c8ef2d804f…
City of Cambridge R-16 IN CITY COUNCIL April 2, 2018 COUNCILLOR SIMMONS COUNCILLOR TOOMEY VICE MAYOR DEVEREUX COUNCILLOR KELLEY COUNCILLOR MAZEN MAYOR MCGOVERN COUNCILLOR MALLON COUNCILLOR SIDDIQUI COUNCILLOR ZONDERVAN WHEREAS: The City Council was saddened to learn of the passing of Kathleen M. “Kay” (Collins) Monagle, mother of City Auditor Jim Monagle, on April 1, 2018 at the age of 87; and WHEREAS: Kathleen M. Monagle was the devoted wife of 67 years to Paul Monagle, the loving mother of Joan Barbosa and her husband Richard, Paul Monagle and his wife Kathleen, James Monagle and his wife Maureen, Richard Monagle and the late Maryalice Monagle, and the cherished grandmother of Brian, Michael, Cameron and Daniel; and WHEREAS: Kathleen M. Monagle’s memory shall be forever cherished by all those who knew her; now therefore be it RESOLVED: That the City Council formally goes on record in expressing its condolences to the loving family of Kathleen M. Monagle for their loss; and be it further RESOLVED: That the City Clerk be and hereby is requested to forward a suitably engrossed copy of this resolution to the family of Kathleen M. Monagle on behalf of the entire City Council. In City Council April 2, 2018. Adopted by the affirmative vote of nine members. Attest:- Donna P. Lopez, City Clerk A true copy; ATTEST:- Donna P. Lopez, City Clerk
