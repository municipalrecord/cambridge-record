City of Cambridge Agenda Item Number 10 IN CITY COUNCIL NOVEMBER 7, 2016 WHEREAS: Section 193 and Section 194 of Chapter 218 of the Acts of 2016 “An Act Modernizing Municipal Finance and Government” gives municipalities the authority to reduce speed limits on all ways other than state highways; and WHEREAS: The acceptance of said Section 193 and Section 194 would authorize the Director of Traffic, Parking and Transportation to promulgate traffic regulations to reduce speed limits as permitted under the Act; now therefore be it ORDERED: That the City Council go on record accepting the provisions of Section 193 and Section 194 of Chapter 218 of the Acts of 2016 “An Act Modernizing Municipal Finance and Government.” In City Council November 7, 2016. Adopted by the affirmative vote of eight members. Attest:- Donna P. Lopez, City Clerk A true copy; ATTEST:- Donna P. Lopez, City Clerk
