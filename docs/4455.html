CITY OF CAMBRIDGE OFFICE OF THE MAYOR mayor@cambridgema.gov Marc C. McGovern Phone: [phone removed] Mayor Fax: [phone removed] To: Cambridge City Council From: Marc C. McGovern, Mayor Date: February 8, 2018 Subject: Adding Chair of Neighborhood & Long-Term Planning; Public Facilities, Arts and Celebrations Committee To the Honorable, the City Council: Due to a mistake on the part of the Mayor's Office, my original communication listed Councillor Dennis Carlone as a member of the Neighborhood & Long-Term Planning; Public Facilities, Arts and Celebrations Committee, but not co-chair. I am hereby announcing my selection of Councillor Carlone to the position of co-chair for that committee. At the request of Councillor Simmons, I am also removing her from Neighborhood & Long-Term Planning; Public Facilities, Arts and Celebrations, and placing Councillor Kelley on the Committee. For your convenience, please find attached an updated list of the Committee assignments. I am also requesting that this letter and attachment be placed on the Monday, February 12, 2018, Regular City Council agenda in Communications and Reports from City Officers. Sincerely, Mine MCe
