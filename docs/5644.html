MOTE NO CASTI OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK CITY OF CAMBRIDGE [phone removed] FAX: [phone removed] DONNA P. LOPEZ PAULA M. CRANE CITY CLERK DEPUTY CITY CLERK DATE: NOVEMBER 15, 2018 TO: THE HONORABLE, THE CITY COUNCIL FROM: DONNA P. LOPEZ, CITY CLERK TO SUBJECT: STATE STATUTES ACCEPTANCE Attached you will find copies of two home rule petitions filed by the City of Cambridge that were passed by the Legislature. They are as follows: Chapter 89 of the Acts of 2017 the same being "AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE CITY OF CAMBRIDGE TO USE CERTAIN LAND ACQUIRED FOR PARK, PLAYGROUND OR RECREATION PURPOSES FOR OTHER MUNICIPAL PURPOSES." Chapter 93 of the Acts of 2018 the same being "AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE APPOINTMENT OF RETIRED POLICE OFFICERS AS SPECIAL POLICE OFFICERS FOR PAID DETAIL ASSIGNMENTS IN THE CITY OF CAMBRIDGE." Please be advised that the two statutes require acceptance by the City Council of the City of Cambridge. I have also attached three proposed orders for adoption by the City Council. Upon adoption of these three orders they will be added to the Statutes Accepted section of the Municipal Code for the next supplement update. Your kind attention in this matter will be greatly accepted. CITY HALL, 795 MASSACHUSETTS AVENUE, CAMBRIDGE, MASSACHUSETTS 02139
