APP 2022 #5 · Agenda item attachment · Feb 28 2022

An application was received from MIT List Visual Arts Center requesting permission for 35 temporary banners across Ames Street Memorial Drive Main Street and Broadway announcing MIT List, New Art on View, Contemporary Art from February 2022 through October 2022. CHARTER RIGHT EXERCISED BY COUNCILLOR CARLONE IN COUNCIL FEBRUARY 28, 2022

Dear City Council of Cambridge, On behalf of the MIT List Visual Arts Center, I am writing to apply for new banners on lamp posts: • Ames St., Memorial Drive to Main St. (16 poles): 21, 19, M19, M17, M15, M13, M9, M7, M5, M3, M1, M2, M4, M6, M8, M10 • Ames St., Main St. to Broadway (8 poles): 6, 4, 2, M41, M42, M44, M52, M54 • Broadway, Ames St. to Third St. (8 poles): M30, M26, M22, M18, M16, M12, M8, M4 • Third St., Broadway to Main St. (2 poles): M25, M23 • Main St., Third St. to Broadway (1 pole): M21 to direct students and public audiences to the contemporary art museum at MIT. The banners say: “MIT List Visual Arts Center, Free and Open to All, The Contemporary Art Museum at MIT, Listart.mit.edu, MITList, New Art on View, Contemporary Art.” with our logo and renderings of our public art, building exterior, and exhibition artworks. We intend to attach the banners this February and remove them in October 2022. Banner attachment/removal will be facilitated by Heritage Flag Co. Thank you for your consideration, Chloe Tomasetta MIT List Visual Arts Center Marketing Associate [phone removed] [email removed]
