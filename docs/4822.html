For a patient to gain access to the Sira retail facility, they must first obtain a medical cannabis certification from a physician (a) who is registered with the MDPH to write medical cannabis certifications; and (b) with whom the patient has a bona-fide patient-physician relationship. A certification specifies an amount ofcannabis medicine a patient may purchase over a certain period of time. vi. Once a patient has acquired a certification, they must register with the MDPH Medical Use of Marijuana Program (MMJ Program) and obtain a MEVfJ Program identification card. The MDPH captures basic patient demographic data at the time of patient registration and shares that data with RMDs for patient verification purposes. w. When a patient arrives at the Sira Naturals Cambridge facility, they must present their MMJ Program ID and another government-issued photo identification to a Sira dispensary agent who verifies (a) that they are an active registrant in the MMJ program; and (b) that they have an allotment of cannabis medicine remaining on their certification. The Sira dispensary agent does this by fogging into the MDPH interoperabte database to check the patient's status in real time. Once verified, the patient is admitted to the facility. vdi. VARIETY OF CBD & THC OPTIONS A growing body of research indicates that the numerous therapeutic effects of cannabis and cannabis-derived products are a function of the synergistic effects of the various cannabinoids within the plant, the two most well-known of which are THC and CBD. THC is primarily responsible for the psychoactive effects associated with cannabis and is often used to treat chronic pain, insomnia, depression and nausea. CBD on the other hand, produces no psychoactive effects and is reported by patients to possess anxiolytic, antispasmodic and anti-inflammation properties. Different ratios of cannabinoids are more suited to treatment of some conditions than others - by offering strains with varying concentrations/rafios of CBD and THC, patients with different conditions are more likely to find a specific strain or product that offers the best relief for their condition.
