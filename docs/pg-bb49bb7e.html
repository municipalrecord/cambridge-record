To: Cambridge City Council From: Sumbul Siddiqui, Mayor Date: May 28, 2026 Subject: Updates to the 2026-2027 City Council Committee Appointments OFFICE OF THE MAYOR Sumbul Siddiqui Mayor (2020-21, 2022-23, 2026-27) To the Honorable, the City Council: I am writing to formally notify you of the following committee assignment changes, effective June 1, 2026. At the request of Councillors Simmons and McGovern, the following changes are being made: • Councillor Simmons will be removed from the Housing Committee and added to the Economic Development & University Relations Committee • Councillor McGovern will be removed from the Economic Development & University Relations Committee and added to the Housing Committee Councillors Simmons and McGovern requested this change to better align their respective council work with their policy priorities. After reviewing this request, I am satisfied that this reassignment serves the best interests of the Council and the residents we represent. A full updated list of committee assignments is attached for your reference. Thank you for your continued service to the residents of Cambridge. Sincerely, Sumbul Siddiqui Mayor of Cambridge (2020-21, 2022-23, 2026-27)
