A communication transmitted from Louis A. DePasquale, City Manager, relative to Awaiting Report Item Number 16-88, regarding identifying an appropriate location for a suitable remembrance for Dorothy Steele at Cambridge Health Alliance

MEMORANDUM Date: November 10, 2016 To: Lisa Peterson, Interim City Manager City of Cambridge From: Patrick R. Wardell, CEO Cambridge Health Alliance RE: Response to POR 2016 O-9 Thank you for bringing POR 2016 #O-9 to my attention regarding determining a location of remembrance to Dorothy Steele at the CHA Cambridge Hospital. In recognition of Ms. Steele’s love for nature and outdoors, Cambridge Health Alliance will be dedicating a tree by the CHA Cambridge Hospital’s main entrance. A plaque, with etchings of birds, will be installed by the tree in remembrance to Ms. Steele. A picture of the tree is shown below: A ceremony dedicating the tree in her memory is tentatively set for the one-year anniversary of her death on March 15, 2017 with weather permitting. Thank you and please do not hesitate to contact me if you should have any additional questions.
