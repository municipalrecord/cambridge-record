City of Cambridge Sidewalk Business Use Petition SBU-060447-2017 To the honorable, the City Council of the City of Cambridge The undersigned respectfully pray 7/27/2017 Applied: 8/2/2017 Crema Cafe located at Marley Brush 27 Brattle St Cambridge, 02138 Name of Petitioner or Business Address Be granted permission for a/an Coffee Trike: Bicycle selling iced coffee, pre-packaged cookies, and pre-packaged dog biscuits. Corral Seating?: Is Alcohol Being Served?: No No Coffee Trike - Bicycle with cold brew and nitro keg system, selling iced coffee, pre-packaged cookies, and pre-packaged dog biscuits. Please find the specific dimensions of the Cargo Bicycle, including Stepped 5-Gallon Tricycle Box and Frame in the document titled Exhibit A in the License Commission Memorandum. The Cargo Bicycle will be stationed with at least three feet of space on all sides of the Trike to accommodate pedestrian and vehicular traffic. When on the sidewalk at least four feet of sidewalk will remain open. Crema Café proposes to sell Nobl Cold Brew and Nitro Iced Coffee and pre-packaged Crema Café cookies and dog biscuits within the City of Cambridge as listed in Exhibit B in the License Commission Memorandum. The Trike will be operated by a Crema Café employee during the caf s business hours of 7AM-9PM, Monday-Friday, and 8AM-9PM, Saturday and Sunday. The Stepped 5-Gallon Tricycle Box will contain two standard coffee kegs measuring 9 in diameter and 23 in height. See attached memorandum. Public Works - Pending City Council approval, the DPW is prepared to issue a Sidewalk Business Use Permit for this request. The DPW will require that, while the coffee trike is on the sidewalk, at least 4ft of open sidewalk must be maintained for pedestrians to pass. Police Department - Pending City Council approval.
