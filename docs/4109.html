Field by Field Permitted Usage Data In the report, situated by field, the following terms are used to define Athletic Field use: “Reservation” identifies the reserving party or organization. “Hours Booked” identifies the total number of hours booked during the time period of April 1st 2017 through November 30th 2017. A small number of changes and accommodations for new permits may be made in the remainder of 2017, but most of bookings for the year have been made at the time of this report. (Note: this number does not include field use by Cambridge Public Schools during the school day for Physical Education or use by DHSP afterschool programs.) “Cambridge Based” identifies the percentage of people participating in permitted use that either work or live in Cambridge. Some agencies do not track this data and were awarded 0% affiliation to Cambridge. “Age Group” identifies the total number of hours permitted to youth serving groups, school groups, and groups serving adults only.
