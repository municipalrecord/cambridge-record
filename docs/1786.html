Our office received notification and a response from the City Couricil and City Clerk on January 27, 2016. · Under the Open Meeting Law, a complaint is ripe for review by our office when the complainant files a copy of the initial complaint with the Division of Open Government, provided that at least 30 days have passed since that complaint was filed with the public bqdy. G.L. c. 30A, § 23(b); 940 CMR 29.05(6).
