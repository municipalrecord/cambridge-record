Charter Review Committee — Final Report, January 31, 2024

Delivered to the City Council and referred to the Government Operations Committee (POR 2024 #19). The committee voted unanimously to draft a new modern charter; on the form of government it split 8–7 between Mayor/CAFO/Council and Council/Manager.
