# School Committee portrait credits

All photos are official Cambridge Public Schools portraits from member pages
on cpsd.us, recovered via the Wayback Machine or the district's SharpSchool
CDN (cdnsm5-ss5.sharpschool.com / cdn5-ss5.sharpschool.com, which serve
cpsd.us's `/UserFiles/Servers/Server_3042785/` media). Displayed credit line:
"Cambridge Public Schools". Files are resized to ≤256 px wide (site standard);
sources below are the originals. Collected 2026-07-21.

## Student members — initials only, by policy

Student representatives (e.g. the 2021–22 student members) render as
initials with no name and no photo anywhere on the site — they are
minors, and their non-binding roll-call positions are shown alongside
the members' votes from the `students` column of votes_school.csv.
Portraits previously collected for them were removed July 22, 2026.

## Not found — still render as initials

- **Akriti Bhambi** (student member): her cpsd.us member page was archived
  (2022-01-05, 2022-12-08) but never carried a portrait, and no image file
  matching her name was archived from any CPSD host.
- **Escamilla-Salomon** (student member): no cpsd.us School Committee member
  page or portrait archived; only unrelated CRLS class-news photos exist.
- **Keady-Rawson** (Sheila Keady-Rawson, the City of Cambridge personnel/HR
  director) and **Catchings** (School Committee office staff) are not members;
  they appear in some parsed vote rows because the minutes parser mis-attributes
  staff present at the meeting as voters (8 yeas on a 7-member committee, e.g.
  2020-05-22 #20-149, 2025-02-04 #25-15). That is a parser bug to fix, not a
  missing portrait.
